Downloading & installing

  1. Download the Zipfile for your OS

    • Linux
    • Mac
    • Windows
  2. Unzip the Zipfile anywhere and then change into the data-kitchen folder
  3. Run the data-kitchen executable (e.g. ./data-kitchen or data-kitchen.exe or data-kitchen.app)
  4. You may edit the settings in the Kitchen/Settings menu. See the in-app Help for details
You can also clone or fork the repo
